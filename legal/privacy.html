Privacy Policy

MobiPass mobile application · Last updated: 2 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mobipass SRL ("MobiPass", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use the MobiPass mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to processing your data lawfully and transparently in accordance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Romanian law.

In short: The App only collects the information needed to sign you in and provide your company bike benefit. We do not track you, we do not use analytics or advertising, we do not use third-party tracking SDKs, and we do not sell or share your data.

1. Who we are

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

  • Mobipass SRL — Romania
  • Contact: contact@mobipass.eu

MobiPass is a workplace benefit platform that lets employees of participating companies browse, test, and commit to a subsidised e-bike. You typically access the App because your employer has invited you to the benefit programme.

2. Data we collect

We keep data collection to the minimum required to operate the service.

CategoryDataPurpose
Account & authentication Your email address and authentication/session tokens (managed by our authentication provider, Supabase Auth). Sign-in may use a one-time email code, a password, or your organisation's Google Workspace account. To create your account, sign you in, and keep your session secure.
Profile & benefit data Basic profile information such as your name, company, and department, together with the status of your bike benefit (e.g. selected bike, benefit step). Profile information is generally provided by your employer to set up your benefit. To display your profile and let you progress through the bike-benefit journey.

We do not collect location data, contacts, device identifiers for tracking, advertising identifiers, or usage analytics.

3. What we do not do

  • No tracking or profiling across apps or websites.
  • No analytics or telemetry SDKs.
  • No advertising or ad networks.
  • No third-party marketing or tracking SDKs embedded in the App.
  • No sale of personal data, and no sharing for third-party advertising.

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Performance of a contract / provision of the service — to give you access to the App and the bike-benefit programme.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the service secure and functioning (e.g. authentication and fraud prevention).
  • Where your employer administers the benefit, processing also supports the employment/benefit relationship between you and your employer.

5. Service providers (processors)

We use Supabase as our infrastructure provider for authentication, database, and storage. Supabase processes data on our behalf under a data processing agreement, and our project is hosted in the European Union (North EU region). We do not transfer your personal data outside the EU/EEA for the operation of the App. We do not use any other third-party processors for the App's functionality.

6. Data location & retention

Your data is stored within the EU. We retain your account and profile data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account (see Section 8), your personal data is permanently deleted from our active systems, except where we are legally required to retain limited records.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to: access your data; rectify inaccurate data; erase your data; restrict or object to processing; and data portability. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority — in Romania, the National Supervisory Authority for Personal Data Processing (ANSPDCP, dataprotection.ro). To exercise your rights, contact us at contact@mobipass.eu.

Note: where your employer is the controller of certain employment-related records, some requests may be directed to, or coordinated with, your employer.

8. Deleting your account

You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the App (Profile → Account → Delete Account). Account deletion is permanent and irreversible: it removes your account and associated personal data from our active systems. You can also request deletion by emailing contact@mobipass.eu.

9. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Sensitive personal data is encrypted at rest, and access to data is restricted through authentication and row-level security controls.

10. Children

The App is intended for employees of participating organisations and is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

12. Contact

Questions or requests about this policy or your data: contact@mobipass.eu
